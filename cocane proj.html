History of Cocaine

Here is how Cocaine evolved over time including its plant form and when it became a drug.

  • First found in 1451-1512.
  • The drug Cocaine was first made in 1860.
  • Made from a plant from South America.
  • Cocaine was known as a wonder drug.
  • Cocaine came from the coca plant.
  • The coca plant is one of the oldest plants in South America.
  • The coca plant’s cultivation began in the Amazon Rainforest.
  • The coca leaf was used in Inca culture and religious ceremonies.
  • The native people of South America also used the coca leaf.

Cocaine as Medicine

Cocaine for a while was used as a medicine. Here are more facts.

  • Back in 1960, Albert Niemen, a German chemist, separated the actual cocaine from the coca leaf.
  • Carl Koller, an Austrian ophthalmologist, started using cocaine and testing it to see if it can be used as a surgical anesthetic since cataract surgery was done without any anesthetics.
  • Cocaine ended up working as an anesthetic and patients did not cause problems when the surgery was in session.
  • . Pharmaceutical companies started selling cocaine solutions after seeing the success it has been making. Unfortunately, people started dying of overdoses from the cocaine solution.
